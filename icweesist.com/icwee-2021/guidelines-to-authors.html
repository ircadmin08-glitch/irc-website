Guidelines to the Authors

  • Submission of abstract needs to be done electronically via the Conference Mail id energy.icwee@gmail.com or energy.icwee@yahoo.com

  • Authors are requested to submit the Abstract as per the prescribed format (downloads section)

  • After the acceptance of the abstract, the authors will be intimated to register for the conference

  • On Acceptance of the Abstract, at least one of the authors MUST register on or before the deadline along with filled in registration form and payment

  • Certificate will be provided only for the registered participant.

  • Upon successful registration, the paper must be presented in the conference according to the Program Schedule. Failure to do so will result in exclusion of the paper during the publication process.

  • For publication in journals, all papers have to be submitted through the submission page in the respected journals. All details regarding paper submission will be communicated to the corresponding author e-mail ID (provided in the abstract) on date of the conference.

  • For conference proceedings publication, kindly prepare manuscript as per prescribed format (downloads section).

  • Publication charges extra (if applicable in cases like if you wish for open access, colour print, proceedings copy, etc).

  • During Payment via Wire Transfer (Netbanking) kindly mention ICWEE-2021 after your name (Ex: XXYYZZ ICWEE-2021
